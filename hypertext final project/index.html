The Robbery That Backfired

By : Vishal Emani


Chapter 1 : The Plan

12:39am, Paterson Road, Orchard, Singapore. Max and Jake, two inexperienced robbers, were hiding in a poorly lighted alleyway in the middle of Orchard Road, the flashy and glamorous area of central Singapore. They were lured in by the promise of quick money, and after weeks of reconnaissance, they had found their target: an opulent mansion subtly tucked away among the posh homes. They carefully planned every stage of the theft as they unfolded their design, hands shaking and hearts pounding. They saw everything going smoothly, seeing themselves as the night's masterminds, and they were blind to the cruel twist of events that was about to happen.
