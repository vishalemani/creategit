Chapter 4 : The Confrontation

Max and Jake,cornered and terrified, tried to run away, but Inspector Tan was one step ahead. He moved quickly to disarm them, showing no mercy with his icy stare. The seriousness of their deeds dawned on them as they stood there, vanquished and captured. Blinded by the prospect of quick money, they had set out on a criminal career, only to discover how foolish they had been.
