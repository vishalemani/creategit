The Robbery That Backfired

By : Vishal Emani


Chapter 1

On a cold, desolate night, two robbers set out to try their luck and attempt to burgle in the poshest part of town. Unfortunately, to their dismay, they are unable to get access to any place. After multiple futile attempts, they finally stumble across a palatial house. Due to the size of the house, they found a way to get into it.
