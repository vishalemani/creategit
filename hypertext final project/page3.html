Chapter 3 : The Unexpected Encounter

Their hearts beat in their chests as they carefully tiptoed down the dimly illuminated halls. But as they got to the opulent lobby, the quiet was broken by a voice that said, "Freeze! You're under arrest!" Taken aback, Max and Jake returned to see that it was none other than Inspector Tan, a well-known member of the Singapore Police Force. They realized that they had made a serious error and had mistakenly targeted the residence of a decorated law enforcement officer, which sent chills down their spine.
