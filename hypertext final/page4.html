Chapter 4

As they were going about their robbing spree, they found a room which was dimly lit. It was filled with portraits of people who looked like they belonged to the Victorian Era. In the middle of the room, there was a glass casing which was more lit than the rest of the room. They inched forward in apprehension, curious to find out what was in that glass casing. As they drew nearer and laid their eyes on the glass casing, they let out a gasp in unison. In it was housed a very precious gemstone, shimmering in all its glory. Their greed outweighed their sense logic and one of them proceeded to grab it from the casing. The moment he did that, the whole room was filled with a deafening noise. They had set off the alarms.
