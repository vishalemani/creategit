Chapter 2 : The Nightfall

Max and Jake slyly approached their target under the cover of darkness, hiding their features behind ski masks. Their breaths were shallow with anticipation as they neared the mansion's formidable gates, adrenaline coursing through their veins. They picked the lock with expert accuracy and slipped inside, their senses heightened by the pleasures of committing a crime. They had no idea, however, that fate had another plan for them, one that would put their resolve to the test and permanently change the path of their lives.
