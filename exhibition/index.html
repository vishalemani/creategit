Digital Reflections: Feelings in the Virtual Environment

Curated by Vishal Emani

In a time when virtual spaces are increasingly shaping and reflecting our real-world experiences, "Digital Reflections: Feelings in the Virtual Environment" aims to investigate the complex relationship that exists between virtual environments and human emotions. A variety of net art pieces that explore the nuanced ways that online spaces shape, reflect, and alter our emotional experiences are included in this exhibition. The featured works explore the complex relationship between our inner sensations and the digital landscapes we traverse, from the loneliness of digital contemplation to the shared delight found in virtual communities. The show attempts to create a deeper understanding of how the digital age transforms our emotional worlds by combining interactive experiences, visual narrative, and conceptual art.
